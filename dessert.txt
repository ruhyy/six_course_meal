Gateau chocolat

Ingredients:
1. 2/3 cup butte
2. 1-2/3 cups Sugar 
3. 3  eggs
4. 2 cups all-purpose flour
5. 1/2 tsp vanilla extract 
6. 1-1/4 teaspoons baking soda
7. 1 teaspoon SALT AGAIN?
8. 1-1/3 cups milk
9. 2 cups molten chocolate 

Directions:
In a bowl, cream butter and sugar until fluffy. 
Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. 
Combine flour, cocoa, baking soda and salt; 
add to creamed mixture alternately with milk, beating until smooth after each addition. 
Pour batter into a greased and floured 13x9-in. pan.
Bake at 350Â° for 35-40 minutes or until cake tests done.
Cool on a wire rack. When cake is cool, dust with confectioners' sugar, or frost with your favorite frosting.
